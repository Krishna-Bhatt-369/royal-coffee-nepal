About Us
We are delighted to introduce our Specialty Coffee plantation situated 55Km. North of our capital Kathmandu which also places us as the northernmost coffee project in the entire northern hemisphere. The Himalayan Country of Nepal is well known for its beautiful & highest mountains in the world. This is why we opened for business as the first & only coffee plantation in this vast Himalayan belt. This makes us the only producer and exporter of superior quality Himalayan snow water washed shade grown/organic/sundried green beans from the Himalayas.
Royal Himalayan Coffee is one of the ground-rooted companies that has been successfully working in partnership with green coffee producers, roasters and exporters throughout the Nepal. Over generations of coffee expertise and knowledge, Royal Himalayan Coffee aims to be recognized as leading business partner in the roasted and green coffee farm. Our goal is to achieve an organic coffee economy by encouraging corporation to all fragments of the coffee industry, from coffee producers to consumers.   
At Royal Himalayan Coffee, we enthusiastically connect coffee producers and roasters to the best specialty Organic green coffee beans for sale.Furthermore, we take great pride to work with our coffee producers and roasting partners as experienced green coffee consultants.
