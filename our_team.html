Meet Our Team

 
Shanta Khanal - Chairman 
Mrs. Shanta has been continuously leading the coffee business for the past 10 years and is a chairman for Royal Aroma Himalayan Coffee. She is creating employment for young youth and has been able to generate a healthy return on investments. She aspires to become one of the inspiring women entrepreneurs and contribute to overall coffee-based national economy creation in Nepal. 

 
Kedar Sapkota (Madan) - Managing Director 
Mr. Kedar is with more than 10 years of experience in the coffee industry. He has wide experience in leading the business on a correct path through his outstanding leadership skills. He strongly believes that the coffee itself can do a lot to uplift the national economy of the country and increase the overall living standard of Nepalese farmers. He has been working especially on value chain based business and is also the current board member for Coffee Producer Association of Nepal. 
 
Tekraj Giri - Senior Technician
Mr. Tek Raj Giri is a senior technician who has more than 12 years of experience in the agricultural sector. He worked for several INGOs and other reputed organization as a technical adviser related to agriculture. He is very much dedicated to his professional career as he always aims to help agriculture foster in Nepal.
