Great Coffee From Nepal

Royal Himalayan Coffee Company grows 100% Highland Specialty Arabica Nepali Coffee. We grow our beans above 1300 m. from sea level which around 55 km north of capital city Kathmandu known as Sindhupalchwok.
It is to be noted that above this altitude due to the proximity to the high Himalayan range , heavy frost in winter would adversely effect the coffee trees.

About us

Royal Himalayan Coffee (RHC) is one of the ground-rooted companies that has been successfully working in partnership with green coffee producers, roasters and exporters throughout the Nepal. Over the generation of coffee expertise and knowledge, RHC aims to be recognized as leading business partner in the roasted and green coffee field.
Our goal is to achieve an organic coffee economy by encouraging corporation to all fragments of the coffee industry, from coffee producers to consumers.   
At RHC, we enthusiastically connect coffee producers and roasters to the best specialty Organic green coffee beans for sale. Furthermore, we take great pride to work with our coffee producers and roasting partners as experienced green coffee consultants.
